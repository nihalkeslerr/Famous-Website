ABOUT US

Famous Art Gallery , founded by Nihal Selma KESLER in 1993 in Beyoğlu, hosted hundreds of exhibitions with the leading names of Turkish Art in its first years. One of the first galleries of Turkey’s contemporary art scene, Famous Art Gallery, in the 1990’s pioneered in exhibiting the first works of artists who would go on to become known names in art in Turkey. As a continuation to the care in the importance of art practices evolving from grassroots beginnings, in recent years again, the gallery gravitated to support freshly up and coming artists.

In order to be closer to the gallery gathering center, Pg Art Gallery moved from Bebek to one of Istanbul’s historic city centers, Tophane, in 2010. Even though the focus is still Turkey, as part of Pg Art Gallery’s internationalization, as off the mid-2000’s, it started to take part in international art fairs and developed a series of cooperations with like-minded galleries and business partners.
